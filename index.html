Ed Viesturs

The only American to climb all 14 of the world's 8K peaks

Here is a timeline of Ed Viesturs' accomplishments:

  • 1959 - Born: Fort Wayne, IN
  • 1981 - Graduated from University of Washington
  • 1989 - Ascended Kangchenjunga
  • 1990 - Ascended Mount Everest
  • 1992 - Ascended K2
  • 1992 - Rescued Chantal Mauduit during ascent of K2 with fellow climber, Scott Fischer
  • 2005 - Became the first American, and 12th person overall, to summit all 14 mountains over 8,000 meters

"Getting to the top is optional. Getting down is mandatory."

— Ed Viesturs
